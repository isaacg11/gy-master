How it works

Complete Assessment

On the initial visit to your home, our Yardners will come out to your residence and check out the condition of your yard, test the soil, and measure the square footage. In addition, they'll assess for seasonality and match the produce that best fits the climate.

Select Your Garden

Most people want to garden. The challenge can be maintaing one. We give you two options: a Yarden Garden or an Agri-space. With a Yarden Garden we'll maintain, harvest, and deliver your produce to you. If you don't eat much produce but want to earn some extra dough, you can opt for an Agri-space and we'll select a crop to be planted, harvested, & sold on the market while you sit back and collect a check.

Keep Updated With The Yarden App

Leverage Yarden's real-time app notifications to keep up-to-date with your garden's maintenance calendar, health, and harvest cycles. Connect through our community forum and meet other Yardeneers in your community. Watch your crops grow and track the progress on your phone.

Our mission

Yarden is about focusing on a global initiative, but taking a local approach. We combine the modern technology with smart, passoinate, service. We believe through small community gardens and micro-farms we can decrease hunger, reduce pesticide usage, and encourage healthy organic eating.

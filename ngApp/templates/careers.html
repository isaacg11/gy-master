What is Yarden?

Yarden is an app that connects gardeners with people who have gardens and request service through our app, and customers pay automatically through the app.

Whether you are saving for a vacation, catching up on some bills, or funding your dreams, Yarden will help you get there. So, be a Yardener. Be your own boss.

How you can earn

- Basic gardening tasks (pruning, watering, etc)

- Customer tips

Apply Now!

What it's like to work with Yarden

Open the app and tap "New Tasks"

Select and accept a task from the list

Drive to garden location on scheduled date/time

Complete the given task per app instructions

Recieve a mobile payment for the work completed!

The App

Gardeners at your fingertips

Yarden App is a platform like "Uber" for gardening and small farming. It connects you to a network of local professionals who plant, maintain, and harvest the garden you always wanted, but never had time for. You'll have access to a variety of cool features including harvest forecasting, weekly growth updates, and much more!
